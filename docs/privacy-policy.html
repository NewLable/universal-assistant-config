Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Privacy at a glance: Universal Assistant Pro does not require an account or login, does not contain advertising, and does not use AI services. Most functionality is performed locally on your device.

1. Introduction

Universal Assistant Pro ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

2. Accounts and Personal Information

Universal Assistant Pro does not require account registration, login credentials, a username, or a password.

The App is designed so that most tools can be used without providing personally identifiable information.

3. Information Entered Into Tools

Information you enter into calculators, converters and other tools is used to perform the function you requested.

Most calculations are performed locally on your device. The App does not operate a user account system for storing this information on a personal online profile.

4. Device Permissions and Sensors

Certain tools may request access to device capabilities when those capabilities are required for the selected feature.

  • Location: may be used by Compass, Altimeter and Barometer features.
  • Magnetometer: used by the Compass feature.
  • Motion sensors: used by Level and inclination measurement tools.
  • Camera flash: used by Flashlight, Strobe Light, SOS Signal and Morse Code tools.

Permissions are requested only when necessary for the relevant functionality and are subject to the permission controls provided by your operating system.

5. Internet Connection and Online Data

Most features of Universal Assistant Pro work offline. Certain features require an Internet connection to retrieve current information from external data providers.

Depending on the feature being used, this may include information such as fuel prices, currency exchange rates, cryptocurrency prices, precious metal prices, or weather-based atmospheric data.

6. External Data Services

The App may communicate with external data services in order to provide specific online features.

These services may include:

  • OpenVan.camp — fuel price information
  • ExchangeRate-API — currency exchange rate information
  • Frankfurter — currency exchange rate information
  • CoinGecko — cryptocurrency market information
  • Gold-API — precious metal price information
  • Open-Meteo — weather-based atmospheric information

When an online feature is used, network requests are required to retrieve the requested information. External providers may process technical information associated with a network request according to their own privacy practices.

7. Artificial Intelligence

Universal Assistant Pro does not use artificial intelligence services to provide its functionality.

8. Advertising and Payments

The current version of Universal Assistant Pro does not contain advertising, subscriptions, in-app purchases, premium upgrades, or payment processing.

9. Local Storage

Application preferences, favorites, history and other settings required for app functionality may be stored locally on your device.

Removing the App may also remove locally stored application data, subject to the behavior of your operating system and device backups.

10. Children's Privacy

Universal Assistant Pro is a general-purpose utility application and is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly operate a system for collecting personal information from children.

11. Data Security

Reasonable measures are used to protect information processed by the App. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the App or its services change. Updates will be published on this page together with a revised "Last updated" date.
