Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

  • Error and crash reports.

The application does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information unless required for a specific feature that you choose to use.

Some features require an Internet connection. The application may communicate with external services to retrieve information or provide requested functionality.

Third-Party Services

The application may use trusted third-party services, including but not limited to:

Data Storage

Most application data is stored locally on your device. If cloud synchronization becomes available, only the information necessary to provide the service will be transmitted securely.

Advertising

The current version of Universal Assistant does not display advertisements. If advertising is introduced in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Children's Privacy

The application is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect information processed by the application. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including access, correction, or deletion where applicable.
